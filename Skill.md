# Skill.md

## Project Objective

Ship a fast, clean, and visually strong website foundation that can be extended
without major refactoring.

## Build Standards

- Keep the core stack simple: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
- Prioritize readability and predictable structure.
- Prefer reusable sections and consistent spacing.
- Ensure mobile and desktop layouts both feel intentional.
- Keep motion subtle and meaningful.

## Quality Checklist

- Page renders without console errors.
- Typography and colors are consistent across sections.
- Buttons and links are visible and keyboard accessible.
- Layout does not break on small screens.
- Any new section follows existing naming conventions.
